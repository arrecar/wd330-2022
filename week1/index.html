Notes from video

Mobile first design
  • Design on the smallest and least powerful device to focus on core functionalities and main content.
  • We now need to design for several type of devices of screen sizes and resolution(desktop, mobile, tablet, etc)
  • Whatever you do, do mobile first

Mobile inputs or forms

  • Make it simple so people can use it easily
  • Problem with forms is the amount of information requested from the user
  • Labels and inputs will make the form compacted or full. You can ditch the labels and just have the inputs display a placeholder
